	The antennas being only 3.35 cm apart can influence each other slightly. The DWM1001C uses UWB channel 5 around 6.5 GHz, where the wavelength is roughly:
		wavelength = speed of light / frequency
		wavelength = 3e8 / 6.5e9
		wavelength ≈ 0.046 m = 4.6 cm
	So the antennas are less than one wavelength apart. That means they are close enough that there can be some mutual coupling or radiation pattern influence.
